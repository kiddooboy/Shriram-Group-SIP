# Shriram Group SIP

**Employer-Sponsored Systematic Investment Plan for Shriram Group Employees**

A voluntary, goal-linked, persona-personalized investment product for 80,000+ employees across all Shriram Group companies — layered on top of EPF, not replacing it.

---

## What Makes This Different

**Shriram already knows its employees.** Most investment platforms start with a blank form and ask you to describe yourself. This platform starts with what Shriram's HRMS already holds — your age, salary band, grade, city, years of service — and uses an AI engine to compute a personalized recommendation *before* you open the app.

The employee sees:
> *"We suggest Rs. 4,500/month in Shriram Multi Asset Allocation Fund for 12 years — based on your income, 2 dependents, and education goal. Projected corpus: Rs. 14.2 lakh."*

They answer 2 quick questions (family situation, primary goal), then either confirm in 1 tap or adjust. **Total onboarding: under 3 minutes.**

The AI recommends: which Shriram AMC fund, what monthly amount, for how long — and explains why in the employee's own language.

## Product at a Glance

| Dimension | Details |
|---|---|
| **Target Users** | All salaried employees across Shriram Group entities |
| **Investment Vehicle** | Shriram AMC mutual fund schemes (Shriram Group's own fund house) |
| **Enrollment Model** | Auto-enrolled (opt-out); default Rs. 500/month |
| **Minimum SIP** | Rs. 500/month |
| **Fund Universe** | Shriram AMC schemes across equity, multi-asset, hybrid, debt, and liquid categories |
| **Goals Supported** | Emergency corpus, short-term, education/home, wealth creation, retirement |
| **Regulatory Stack** | SEBI, AMFI, RBI (NACH), Income Tax Act, EPFO-additive, DPDP Act 2023 |
| **Platform** | Mobile app (iOS/Android) + Web portal + WhatsApp bot |
| **Languages** | 7 vernacular languages (Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Marathi, Bengali, Malayalam) |

## Rollout Phases

| Phase | Timeline | Scope | Gate |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1 — Pilot** | Months 1–9 | Shriram Finance + 1 subsidiary (~5,000 employees) | >55% enrollment, <10% SIP bounce, NPS >40 |
