# Shriram Group SIP — Employee Persona Playbook

Six archetypes derived from Shriram Group's workforce demographics (80,000+ employees across NBFCs, auto finance, insurance, and chit fund entities in 18 states).

---

## How Personas Are Assigned

**Persona Quiz (5 questions at onboarding):**
1. What is your age band? (22–28 / 28–35 / 35–45 / 45–55 / 55–60)
2. What is your approximate monthly in-hand salary? (ranges)
3. Do you have financial dependents? (none / spouse / children / parents / multiple)
4. What is your primary financial goal right now? (emergency fund / saving for a goal / building wealth / retirement)
5. How would you react if your SIP value dropped 20%? (I'd stop / I'd worry but continue / I wouldn't worry much / I'd invest more)

System auto-assigns a persona. Employee can review and override their assignment before completing onboarding.

## Persona 1 — "The Starter" (Aarav)

**Archetype:** Young professional, early career, Tier 2/3 city

### Profile
| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Age | 22–28 |
| Income | Rs. 3–5 LPA (Rs. 20,000–35,000/month in-hand) |
| Role | Field sales, operations, customer service, branch staff |
| Location | Tier 2/3 city (Coimbatore, Nagpur, Kochi, Bhubaneswar) |
| Family | Single or recently married; minimal dependents |
| Existing Savings | EPF (mandatory); possibly a recurring deposit |
| Financial Awareness | Low — may not have heard of mutual funds |
| Tax Regime | Likely new tax regime (income below Rs. 7 lakh → zero tax with standard deduction) |

### Financial Goals
1. Emergency corpus (3–6 months expenses)
2. Marriage savings (1–4 years)
3. First vehicle (2–3 years)
4. Early wealth habit formation

### Risk Appetite: Low-Moderate
- Anxious about losing money; may have seen family members lose money in risky instruments
- Needs reassurance and simple, visual explanations

### Recommended Fund Mix

**Old Tax Regime (if applicable):**
| Fund | Allocation | Category |
|---|---|---|
| Nifty 50 Index Fund | 50% | Large Cap Passive |
| ELSS | 20% | Tax Saving Equity |
| Liquid Fund | 30% | Capital Protection |

**New Tax Regime (recommended for this income band):**
| Fund | Allocation | Category |
|---|---|---|
| Balanced Advantage Fund | 70% | Dynamic Asset Allocation |
| Liquid Fund | 30% | Capital Protection |

### SIP Range
- **Minimum:** Rs. 500/month
- **Suggested:** Rs. 1,000/month (split: Rs. 700 Balanced Advantage + Rs. 300 Liquid)
- **Auto-escalation:** Rs. 100 increase per year or 50% of annual increment (whichever lower)

### Behavioral Nudges
| Trigger | Message |
|---|---|
| Onboarding | "Think of SIP as an EMI you pay to your future self." |
| First SIP success | "Rs. 1,000 invested today = Rs. 5.2 lakh in 15 years at 12% CAGR." |
| Market dip | "When market falls, your SIP buys more units. You're actually getting a discount!" |
| 6-month milestone | "You've built Rs. X as a habit. Your emergency fund is taking shape." |
| Salary revision | "Your salary grew — should your SIP grow with it?" |

### Communication Preferences
- **Primary:** WhatsApp (vernacular language)
- **Secondary:** SMS
- **Language:** Regional (Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Hindi, Marathi, Bengali, Malayalam)
- **Content format:** Short video (2 min), infographic, voice message in regional language

3. Healthcare corpus (accumulation phase)

| Fund | Allocation (Age 45) | Allocation (Age 55) |

| Large Cap Index Fund | 25% | 15% |

- **Additional:** Annual bonus lump-sum into short duration debt

| Trigger | Message |

| Annual | Retirement readiness score: "At current pace, you'll retire with Rs. X. Your target is Rs. Y. Gap: Rs. Z." |
| Age 50 | "10 years to target retirement. Time to review your glide path — we've shifted 5% from equity to debt automatically." |

| Market rally | "Your portfolio made good gains. Consider locking in some profits into debt to protect your retirement corpus." |

### Communication Preferences
- **Primary:** Email + mobile app

## Persona Assignment Override Rules

1. If employee answers income-band question that conflicts with assigned persona, income band takes precedence.
2. Employee can manually move up/down one persona band.
3. Persona is reviewed annually (trigger: birthday month) with a 3-question refresh quiz.
4. Major life events trigger persona re-assessment notification: marriage, childbirth, home purchase, divorce.
